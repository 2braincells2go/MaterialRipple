Materialripple

jQuery-Plugin for the Material Design Ripple Effect

I'm happy about your interest in Materialripple - the jQuery-plugin for the famous Material Design active-effect. With this plugin, you can use the active-effect in your projects too without doing anything! The plugin does everything important for you.

If you want to learn more about Materialripple, check out the documentation below.
